Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026 · Effective: March 31, 2026

Summary: Hydriq stores all your data locally on your device by default. Optional Google Drive backup transmits data only when you explicitly enable it. We do not share personal information with third parties. There are no analytics, no ads, and no tracking.

1. Introduction

Hydriq ("the App") is a hydration tracking application for Android developed by an independent developer ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

By using Hydriq, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

All data you enter is stored on your device by default. If you enable Google Drive backup, a copy of your data is uploaded to your personal Google Drive account (see Section 4).

3. How Your Data Is Stored

  • All data is stored in an encrypted local database (Room/SQLite with SQLCipher AES-256-GCM) on your device.
  • The encryption key is protected by the Android Keystore system.
  • App preferences are stored in local SharedPreferences.
  • The App does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or advertising frameworks.
  • Network requests are made only for: optional Google Drive backup/restore, Google Sign-In authentication, and in-app purchases via Google Play Billing. All network traffic uses HTTPS.

4. Cloud Backup

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

You may optionally sign in with your Google account to back up your hydration data to Google Drive. When enabled:

  • Backups are stored in your Google Drive's appDataFolder — a hidden, app-isolated folder that is not visible in your Drive UI and can only be accessed by Hydriq.
  • Your Google email address is stored locally to maintain the sync session.
  • You can disable backup, delete cloud backups, or sign out at any time in Settings → Data & Privacy.
  • We do not access any other files in your Google Drive — the App uses only the drive.appdata scope.

Android Auto Backup

Android may back up your app preferences (theme, settings) via Google's Auto Backup service. Your hydration database and encryption keys are excluded from Auto Backup to protect your health-related data.

You control Android's backup settings in your device's system settings.

5. Data Export & Portability

The App provides built-in tools for data portability:

  • JSON Backup: Export your complete data (profile + hydration logs) as a JSON file.
  • CSV Export: Export your hydration logs as a CSV spreadsheet.
  • Restore: Import a previously exported JSON backup.

Exported files are saved to your device's Downloads folder or shared via Android's share sheet. We have no access to these files.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  • Individual entries: Swipe to delete any drink log (with undo).
  • All data: Settings → Delete All Data permanently removes all hydration logs and resets your profile.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

8. Third-Party Services

Hydriq integrates with the following Google services only:

  • Google Play Billing — processes in-app purchases (Hydriq Pro, Tip Jar). Payment details are handled entirely by Google Play; the App stores only a purchase status flag locally.
  • Google Drive API — optional cloud backup to your personal Drive (appDataFolder scope only). Used only when you enable Drive backup in Settings.
  • Google Sign-In — authenticates your Google account for Drive backup. Your email is stored locally; no data is sent to our servers.

The App does not use:

  • No analytics (no Firebase, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media integrations
  • No proprietary server-side components — all data stays between your device and your Google account

9. In-App Purchases

Hydriq offers optional in-app purchases:

  • Hydriq Pro ($2.99 one-time) — unlocks cosmetic Spirit skins, outfits, accessories, and themes. No core functionality is gated behind Pro.
  • Tip Jar (3 tiers: $0.49 / $0.99 / $1.49) — optional support tips.

All purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. The App does not store payment card details or financial information.

10. Children's Privacy

Hydriq is not directed at children under 13. The App contains opt-in alcohol tracking (for adult wellness use) and in-app purchases. Parental guidance is recommended. See the health disclaimer at Settings → About & Support → Legal.

Always consult your healthcare provider regarding your hydration needs, especially if you have a medical condition.
